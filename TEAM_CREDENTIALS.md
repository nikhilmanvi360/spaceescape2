## HOW TO USE

1. When Firebase is unavailable, click the **"Team Access"** button
2. Select your team from the list (ALPHA, BETA, or GAMMA)
3. Enter your team's password from the credentials above
4. Click **"Activate"** to authenticate
5. You will now have access to the system in fallback mode

---

## IMPORTANT NOTES

- ⚠️ Team authentication operates in **FALLBACK MODE** only
- All scores will be stored locally when using team credentials
- Team accounts have standard **USER** role permissions
- Passwords are case-sensitive
- Contact admin if you need to reset credentials

---

## TROUBLESHOOTING

**Q: I forgot my team password**
A: Use the password listed above for your team (Alpha, Beta, or Gamma)

**Q: Can I create my own team?**
A: No, only the three predefined teams (Alpha, Beta, Gamma) are available

**Q: What if Firebase comes back online?**
A: You'll be able to switch back to Google authentication. Your fallback scores will be preserved locally.

**Q: Are fallback scores saved to Firebase when it comes back?**
A: No, fallback scores are stored locally. You may need to manually migrate them.
